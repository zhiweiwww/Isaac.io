About The Binding of Isaac

• A 2D plane role playing, action adventure RPG kind of independent game.
• Eleven characters are available in the game.
• There are more than 70 bosses in the game.
• There are more than 400 items to pick up in the game.
• Each time a character picks up a items, the character changes.
