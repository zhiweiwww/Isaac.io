Gemini

Gemini is a leader who joined in rebirth

Mode of action

1.Gemini and Gemini baby act separately and calculate blood volume respectively, but there is umbilical cord between them, which limits the action of Gemini baby.
2.Gemini walking on the ground, chasing characters, can cause contact injury, after a while will stop to rest and gasp.
3.Gemini baby floats in the air and moves slowly towards the character, which can cause contact damage; when the character enters its range, it will fire a tear at the character from time to time.
4.If Gemini is defeated first, Gemini baby will be angry and turn red, no longer shooting tears, but chase the character at high speed; at this time, when the Gemini baby is hit by tears, the repulsion effect will become very obvious, making it difficult to be attacked continuously by the character.
