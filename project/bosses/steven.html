Steven

Steven is a leader in the rebirth.

Mode of action

1.Steven and Steven's baby act separately and calculate blood volume respectively.
2.Steven walks on the ground, chasing characters, can cause contact damage, after a while will stop to rest for breath.
3.Baby Steven floats in the air and moves slowly towards the character, which can cause contact damage; when the character enters its range, it will fire a tear at the character from time to time.
4.When the remaining 50% of Steven's blood, baby Steven will be angry and red, no longer shoot tears, but chase the character at high speed; at this time, when baby Steven is hit by tears, the repulsion effect will become very obvious, making it difficult for him to be attacked continuously by the character.
