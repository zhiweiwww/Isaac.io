Eden

Eden is the combination of Isaac: the new character in rebirth, and the unlocking method is to complete the hotbed for the first time. Eden is unique in that he can only be chosen when he has defeated his mother's heart, he is still alive, or is greedy for the money. Each time you start with Eden, you will consume an Eden coin. According to the current seed, you can generate a random Eden with 1 of 10 hairstyles. You can get random data and get 2 random opening items.
Eden's data is floating on the basis of Isaac, which means that Eden is similar to Isaac, but the data of different Edens may be quite different. On this page you can see that Eden added and subtracted according to Isaac's data, with upper and lower limits. How the system randomizes Eden's data and how it controls Eden that is too strong or too weak is unknown. In addition, each game Eden's hairstyle is different, but only the difference in the display effect.
