Keeper

The keeper is a combination of Isaac: a new hidden character in the placenta DLC. Unlock the owner needs to donate 1000 coins to the greedy machine. The owner starts with a bomb and three tears, and holds a coin (unlock after defeating silence), store key (unlock after defeating Satan) and wooden coin (unlock after defeating Isaac (leader)).
Unlike other characters, the owner's blood is money. The owner of the opening game has two coins. He will lose one coin each time he is attacked. He will recover one coin when he picks up the coin. All types of hearts will turn into blue flies. Shopkeepers can't have more than two coins of blood. When the owner's health is reduced to only one (trading with demons, using less blood), any item that increases health can restore it to two coins.
